10.0.1.1 The Mindset

Your mindset is very important to learning how to learn. Your goal should be to answer an interesting question. Your objective is not to memorize a bunch of functions but to use those functions to do something interesting. The path to accomplishing that goal may be circuitous, and you may experience setbacks before moving forward. That‚Äôs okay!
